DE-CLOSE LOAN DEPARTMENT

Loan Closure means fully repaying the loan and officially closing the loan account with the bank or lender.

Simple explanation:
When you pay:
- the entire remaining principal,
- interest, and
- any applicable charges,
and the bank confirms that no amount is due, the loan is said to be closed (de-closed).

After loan closure, you receive:
- No Dues Certificate (NDC)
- Loan Closure Letter
- Increase CIBIL Score / Credit Report
